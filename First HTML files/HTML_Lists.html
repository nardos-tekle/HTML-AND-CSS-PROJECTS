Ingredients: ♥

  • 1 1/2 cups Pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup White Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 3/4 cups All-purpose flour
  • 2 Egg
  • 1/4 cups Milk
  • 1 1/2 tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup Oil (Canola, Vegetable, Olive, etc.)
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Ginger

Instructions: ♥

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (218°C). Grease a 12-cup muffin pan with nonstick spray or line it with cupcake liners.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, ginger, and salt, whisking until well mixed. Set aside.
  3. In a separate medium bowl, whisk together the oil, granulated sugar, brown sugar, pumpkin puree, eggs, and milk until smooth. Pour this wet mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients, folding gently until just combined and no flour pockets remain.
  4. Fill each muffin cup to the top with the batter. Bake at 425°F for 5 minutes, then lower the temperature to 350°F (177°C) without removing the muffins. Continue baking for another 16–17 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. The total bake time is around 21–22 minutes. Let the muffins cool in the pan for 5 minutes before serving.
